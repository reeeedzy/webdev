Starting Out

Turner Builders, Inc. was formed in December of 1990 and incorporated in 1991 by Mike Turner. Prior to starting his own company Mike was with Overcash Goodman Construction and worked on a wide variety of projects across the United States. Mike is a 1984 graduate of Texas A & M University with a degree in Building Construction. With over 39 years of construction experience, Turner Builders can handle just about any project.

Community Work

In addition to his construction experience, Mike has been heavily involved in the Fort Worth community. Throughout the years Mike has served on the Trinity Habitat for Humanity board and served as President, Secretary, and chair of nominating. Mike has also served on the boards of Tarrant Area Community of Churches, Southside Preservation Hall, and the Suicide Awareness of Fort Worth & Tarrant County. Mike has also been involved with the YWCA of Fort Worth and Tarrant County and was member of the Building Committee for the Renovation of the YWCA facilities in addition to many years of volunteer work. First Presbyterian Church has also been a calling for Mike where he is an Elder and has served on the Mission, Property, Personnel, Nominating, Property, and Building Committees.

Synopsis

Turner Builders, Inc. has built custom homes throughout the Fort Worth area and surrounding communities, remodeled kitchens, bathroom, and whole house remodels. Hail damage, flood damage, and fire related projects have also been completed.
