Education & Experience

Mike is a 1984 graduate of Texas A&M University with a degree in Building Construction. Prior to starting his own company, Mike was with Overcash Goodman Construction and worked on a wide variety of projects across the United States. Turner Builders, Inc. was formed in December of 1990 and incorporated in 1991 by Mike Turner. With over 39 years of construction experience, Turner Builders can handle just about any project.

Diverse History

Turner Builders, Inc. has been building custom homes throughout the Dallas - Fort Worth area for over 30 years! We have also specialized in kitchen, bathroom, and even whole house remodels. Other specialities include hail damage, flood damage, and fire damage repair related projects.
